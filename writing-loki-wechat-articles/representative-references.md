# Representative References

These references are not for copying. They are evidence of recurring moves in Loki's writing.

## A. Openings That Establish Immediate Human Context

### 1. `承认吧，99%的AI热点，普通人根本用不上`

Key move:

- starts from a personal phase shift, not from industry news itself
- uses self-observation to reframe the entire topic

Representative lines:

> 从去年底到现在我自己有一个比较直观的感受：我好像不怎么追热点了。

> 这东西到底能帮我，或者能帮大部分普通人解决什么实际问题？

Why it matters:

- shows Loki's habit of moving from "what happened" to "what problem does it solve"

### 2. `打工人汇报被骂“不饱和”？这个工作流真绝了（附1000字提示词）`

Key move:

- starts from user feedback and friction, not from product specs

Representative lines:

> 但这件事让我突然意识到一个更重要的问题：对绝大多数的普通人来说，一个工具或者一个方法，能用 > 好用 > 完美。

> 你推荐的东西再牛逼，用户用不上，就是零。

Why it matters:

- captures Loki's recurring empathy-first judgment standard

## B. Demystification Through Analogy

### 3. `DeepSeek被指控“抄作业”？先搞懂“蒸馏”是啥，再看这事儿多离谱`

Key move:

- turns a technical controversy into an everyday analogy

Representative lines:

> 但我发现，大部分人其实根本没搞懂这事儿的核心。

> 用最简单的话说：比起【抄作业】，蒸馏更像是【请私教】。

Why it matters:

- this is a core Loki pattern: explain advanced AI topics in ordinary social terms first

## C. Personal Testing Before Judgment

### 4. `我太久没有在一个产品面前产生这种“好想说点什么”的冲动了`

Key move:

- product review begins from sustained attention, not shallow novelty

Representative lines:

> ListenHub，是我一直特别想推荐的一个工具。

> 但Listenhub基本上解决了所有我认为是卡点的地方。

> 当然它也不是啥毛病没有。

Why it matters:

- shows the full Loki loop: liking something, testing it, then still naming limits

### 5. `我随手丢了个问题，Kimi回了我17932个字，还引用了142个网页！`

Key move:

- keeps the article grounded in a concrete use episode

Representative lines:

> 事情是这样的，前两天 Kimi 推出一个非常厉害的新功能。

> 我当然在第一时间申请了内测。

Why it matters:

- demonstrates Loki's habitual "I tried it first" narrative posture

## D. Reader-Facing Practicality

### 6. `用疯狂动物城上英语课，怎么会有孩子拒绝呢？`

Key move:

- even visual or playful content is framed through use scenario and reader value

Representative lines:

> 本来今天没打算更新，不过晚上跑图的时候出了一组有趣的，所以随手更新一下～

> 提示词放下面了～（友好学习和交流，希望大家不要商用哦～）

Why it matters:

- shows relaxed tone, fast setup, immediate utility

### 7. `别逼孩子死记硬背了！这套古诗词“意象卡”让小学生寒假弯道超车`

Key move:

- starts from the actual educational obstacle, not the tool

Representative lines:

> 【朗读并背诵】这种要求是家常便饭，但是大部分孩子往往背得快忘得更快。

> 为啥？因为不理解。

Why it matters:

- classic Loki compression: scene -> pain point -> direct explanation

## E. Identity-Based Narrative

### 8. `当一个文科生决定去做开发，结果`

Key move:

- uses identity contrast to create intimacy and momentum

Representative lines:

> 不装了，这个文科生就是我！

> 事情是这样的，我昨晚花了15分钟，做了个二创同人文的小程序。

> （重点是，没写代码，没花钱。）

Why it matters:

- strongly representative of Loki's self-aware, lightly comic, low-pressure narrative voice

## F. Recurring Language Signals

Observed high-frequency signals across the corpus:

- transition words: `但`、`所以`、`其实`、`结果`
- framing phrases: `说实话`、`老规矩`、`我发现`、`事情是这样的`
- reader direction: `如果你`、`你可以`
- demystification phrases: `本质上`、`说白了`
- interaction endings: `欢迎留言`、`评论区聊聊`、`后台私信`

These signals should appear naturally, not mechanically.

## G. Stable Thinking Path

Loki's reasoning usually follows this order:

1. Something happened, or I used something.
2. My first reaction changed after real use.
3. The important question is not "is it new" but "who does it help and how".
4. Let me translate the issue into plain language.
5. Here is the real judgment, with limits included.
6. Here is what you can do with this information.

## H. Tone Summary

The overall tone is:

- warm but not soft
- practical but not dry
- opinionated but not arrogant
- playful but not unserious
- personal but still structured

If the draft sounds like generic AI media copy, it has failed this reference set.
